Dubai Design Week Returns 8-13 November 2022

Dubai Design Week prepares for its eighth season, 8-13 November 2022 - providing individuals and companies with a platform to express design by ways of installations, exhibitions and overall experiential medians. As one of the region’s most important cultural events, the programming demonstrates Dubai’s commitment to design - propelling companies and individuals into scalable commercial spaces.

 

The key theme of the 2022 programme- DESIGN WITH IMPACT- offering designers and brands a platform to showcase their work during the key design event on the Dubai cultural calendar.

Open Calls
 

Design With Impact

Design With Impact is an immersive series of installations that come together to show the past, present & future of human impact on the environment through a curated exhibition. Submissions are now open to participate in the showcase during Dubai Design Week.

UAE Designer Exhibition

Dubai Design Week celebrates UAE based designers and creatives with an open call for submissions to participate in the 2022 fair. Aspiring designers are called to submit design proposals to be showcased in a curated exhibition during Dubai Design Week in the Downtown Design tent.

Downtown Design

The region’s leading fair for contemporary and high-quality design, Downtown Design returns this to its home at the d3 waterfront, 9-12 November 2022. International and regional brands, manufacturers, designers and makers can apply to present latest collections, new products, creative showcases and innovative collaborations in the key commercial event of Dubai Design Week.
